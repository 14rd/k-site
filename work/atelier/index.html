Atelier

Where it actually happens — toiles, fittings, and the long argument between cloth and pattern.

Year
2024—26
Category
Process · Development
Role
Pattern cutting & fit
Materials
Calico · toile

Most of a garment's life is spent in calico. Each look is toiled, fitted and corrected on the body before a single length of final fabric is cut.

This is the part clients rarely see and the part that matters most: the unglamorous, iterative work of making something fit.

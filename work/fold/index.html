Fold

One rectangle of jersey, folded and pinned on the stand until it resolved into a sleeve.

Year
2024
Category
Draping study
Role
Drape & pattern
Materials
Washed grey jersey

Fold is a draping exercise rather than a flat-pattern one — the piece was developed entirely on the body, then traced back into a clean two-piece pattern.

The asymmetry is deliberate: the drape falls long on one side and breaks at the hip on the other, so the garment changes completely as the wearer turns.

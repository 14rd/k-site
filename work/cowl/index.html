Cowl

A kimono-cut cowl that pools softly at the neck and falls open down the length of the arm.

Year
2024
Category
Knitwear
Role
Design & pattern
Materials
Fine merino · viscose

Knitted in fine merino, the cowl is engineered to drape rather than cling, with the weight of the yarn doing the shaping.

Paired back to a grey mini, it becomes a study in how one relaxed top can read as both loungewear and eveningwear.

Veil

Cape sleeves cut on the cross, falling clean from the shoulder over a quiet column mini.

Year
2025
Category
Eveningwear
Role
Design, pattern & make
Materials
Silk crêpe · fine jersey

The sleeve is a single bias panel that hangs without a side seam, catching the light differently with every step.

Underneath, the dress is stripped right back — a plain tube graded close to the body, so the sleeve can do all the talking.

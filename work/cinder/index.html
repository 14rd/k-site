Cinder

A hooded set in fog grey — drawcord tailoring softened into something you could disappear into.

Year
2025
Category
Knit · Loungewear
Role
Design, pattern & make
Materials
Brushed cotton fleece · washed silk

Cinder started as a study in volume: how much ease a body can carry before a garment stops reading as tailored and starts reading as shelter.

The hood is cut in one with the shoulder; the waist is drawn closed with washed-silk ties. Everything sits in a single fog grey, carried head to hand by a slouched bag in the same cloth.

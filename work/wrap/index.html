Wrap

A wrap mini built from two pattern pieces, draped to hold the waist without a single dart.

Year
2025
Category
Jersey · Dress
Role
Design, pattern & make
Materials
Heavy matte jersey

The wrap is cut so the overlap falls on the bias, letting the jersey skim the body and settle into the waist on its own.

It is the kind of piece that looks effortless and is anything but — most of the work is hidden in the internal stay and the angle of the tie.

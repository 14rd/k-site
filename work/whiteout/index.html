Whiteout

A complete look in white, photographed where it makes the most sense — against fresh snow.

Year
2025
Category
Tailoring · Outerwear
Role
Design & pattern
Materials
Bonded cotton twill · matte poplin

Bonded cotton holds a clean, architectural line at the shoulder while the trouser is left soft, so the silhouette reads sharp from the front and easy from the side.

Tonal dressing is unforgiving: with no colour to hide behind, the whole look lives or dies on proportion and seam placement. That is exactly the point.

Kenna Neyland Fort Collins, CO

I design womenswear and considered pieces — and I cut every pattern myself.

Kenna Neyland is a fashion designer and pattern cutter working between ready-to-wear and made-to-measure. Her work lives in a narrow tonal range — greys, whites and the spaces between — where cut and construction have nowhere to hide.

Trained in both design and garment technology, she takes pieces from first sketch through draping, flat pattern, toile and final make. The result is a practice equally at home with a creative brief and a tech pack.

She works with independent labels and private clients on collection development, pattern cutting and fit — and is always open to studio collaborations.

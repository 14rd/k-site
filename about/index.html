Kenna Neyland Los Angeles, CA

Kenna Neyland is an artist specializing in apparel, accessories, and world-building, working at the intersection of creativity and technology.

Her practice explores the functional basis of art through a brutalist yet human approach, prioritizing process, material honesty, and conceptual depth. With work shown at London and Paris Fashion Week, Discharge Magazine, Dover Street Market, and global military platforms, she pushes the boundaries of what apparel can be through a technical and artistic lens.

Neyland creates in the tension between opposites — structure and fluidity, concept and utility — favoring subtlety and embedded meaning over overt display. Her work resists easy definition, living in the gray areas where contradiction becomes potential and nuance becomes the point.
